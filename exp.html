What I'm involved in


Phi Sigma Rho CSSTARS Girls Who Code

A social sorority built specifically for women studying STEM disciplines who have heavier workloads. We are there for each other through it all, and provide a social safety net for each other. As well as the social benefits, membership provides resume reviews, workshops, and studying with like minded people. I hold two positions, Scholarship director and Alumni Engagement Director. As scholarship director, I encourage professional and academic development through resume workshops, setting up study hours, and resume pools. For Alumni Engagement, I send out newsletters to our alumni and set up events during homecoming and our big alumni gathering in the spring.

Every Sunday, me and other UIUC students spend a few hours teaching young girls essential coding concepts. After a semester of teaching and guiding them through projects to learn, we have them make their own project with the languages they have learned! As a facilitator, I help plan out and teach the activities to the children every week. It is a rewarding way to encourage more women to be interested in STEM concepts, as well as a great test in leadership and mentoring.
